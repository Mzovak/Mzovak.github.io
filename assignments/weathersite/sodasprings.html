Current Weather Story

Reported by SILVER STAR ON MARCH 11, 2019 Last Thursday, Silver Star hosted a “Contractors’ Breakfast” eat and learn event at the Etna Community Center. Seventeen local Star Valley builders were treated to a catered breakfast by the Rolling Omelette, with coffee provided by the Etna Trading Company. Silver Star operations supervisors, engineers and technicians gave presentations on broadband installation guidelines for new construction, the benefits of adding broadband internet to a home, Wi-Fi optimization, integrating smart home devices and more. The learning continued with follow-up question & answer forums and one-on-one discussions. ” Participants were eager to engage and determine best practices for equipment placement and material accrual with Silver Star employees. A great back-and-forth was observed in both educational content and entertaining banter,” reported Abbie Lancaster, Administrative Assistant, Silver Star Communications. Swag bags were given to each attendee, full of fun and useful items such as ice scrapers, travel mugs, flash drives, screen wipes and more! A deluxe camp chair and a Roku Ultra were also handed out to two lucky winners as door prizes! A big thank you to all who attended!
